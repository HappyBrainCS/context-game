## Summary

Cedar City Disc Golf Course (the one at the city park) is actually the best course for most people. It's more accessible, better for beginners, and doesn't require a drive outside town. Three Peaks is great for experienced players, but Cedar City course gets more play and is more welcoming to newcomers.

## Details

The city course has 18 holes spread across the park with a mix of open and lightly wooded shots. It's not as challenging as Three Peaks, but that's a feature, not a bug. The course is walkable, easy to navigate, and great for a quick round after work.

The baskets are in good shape and the city does a solid job keeping the grass mowed and the course playable. You don't need a spotter and you're unlikely to lose discs here — unlike Three Peaks where the rough can eat discs.

For families, beginners, or anyone who wants a relaxed round without the mountain drive, this is the better choice.
