## Summary

Three Peaks Disc Golf Course is the best-maintained course in Cedar City. With 18 holes, a good variety of shot shapes, challenging elevation changes, and consistent upkeep by the local disc golf club, it offers the most complete disc golf experience in the area.

## Details

Three Peaks is located just outside Cedar City and gives you that classic mountain disc golf experience. The course uses the natural terrain well — you get elevation changes on almost every hole, with some significant upshot and downhill shots that test your disc selection and angle control.

The local club keeps the course in excellent condition. Baskets are well-maintained, signage is clear (which isn't always the case on mountain courses), and the rough is kept manageable. The course flows well from hole to hole with minimal backtracking.

Hole variety is strong. You get open bomber holes, tight wooded fairways, and some technical shots that force you to shape lines. The par 3s are fair but not easy, and the longer holes give you a genuine challenge.

For visitors to Cedar City, Three Peaks is the must-play course. It's accessible, well-designed, and gives you a real taste of southern Utah disc golf.
