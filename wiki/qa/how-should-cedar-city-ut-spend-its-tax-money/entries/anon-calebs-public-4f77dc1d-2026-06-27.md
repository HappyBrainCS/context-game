Every town has a budget. Almost none of them have a public record of what residents actually think about how that money should be spent.

Cedar City should put its budget questions on the open record. Residents can answer in one sentence, submit alternatives, and the best ideas rise through blind ranking.

This doesn't replace the city council. It gives them data they've never had before: what do residents actually want?

## How It Works

Create a question for each major budget category. Let anyone submit their priorities. Blind Elo ranking finds the strongest ideas, not the loudest voices.
